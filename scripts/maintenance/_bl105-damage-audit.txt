DAMAGE INTERPRETATION
---------------------
* 42 profile views executed — those don't render template text so zero
  literal-token exposure from step 0.
* 17 connection requests executed on step 1. Per brief [boh2k3qzp], step 1
  is a blank connect (no note) for cold outreach. Literal {FIRSTNAME}
  tokens only ship inside message bodies (step 2+), NOT connection
  requests. These 17 connection-requests likely DID NOT ship broken
  tokens — they were blank connect actions by design.
* 0 message actions completed on any of the 4 campaigns. This is the
  critical datum: NO prospects received a LinkedIn message with literal
  {FIRSTNAME}/{COMPANYNAME} text from these campaigns during this
  damage window.
* Conclusion: pause caught the campaigns BEFORE any connection
  acceptance triggered a message-step send. Zero confirmed literal-token
  messages on wire from these 4 campaigns.
