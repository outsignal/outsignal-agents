## Services & Core Offers

Card machines via Dojo partnership. Freedom Consultants is a Dojo partner/reseller selling Dojo card machines and payment solutions.

**Core Offer:** 30-day free trial -- run Freedom's tech side-by-side with the current setup. If not faster or easier, full refund of fees. If the client loves it, Freedom pays the current provider's exit fees.

**Pricing & Sales Cycle:** Most owners feel stuck in contracts with slow tech. The trial removes risk entirely -- no cost to switch, no penalties.
