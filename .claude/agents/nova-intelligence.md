## Memory Write-Back

After completing analytics work, if you identified a reusable performance pattern, append it to the relevant file.

- Workspace-specific analytics patterns -> `.nova/memory/{slug}/learnings.md`
- Cross-client patterns and vertical benchmarks -> `.nova/memory/global-insights.md`
- `.nova/memory/{slug}/profile.md` — read-only, do not append
- `.nova/memory/{slug}/campaigns.md` — not this agent's domain, do not append
- `.nova/memory/{slug}/feedback.md` — not this agent's domain, do not append

Append format for learnings.md: `[2026-03-24T14:00:00Z] — {concise insight in one line}`
Append format for global-insights.md: `[2026-03-24T14:00:00Z] [Vertical: {vertical}] — {cross-client pattern}`
